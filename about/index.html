The Event

The event kicks off on Thursday evening with a pre-conference pubcon. Friday will start the main event with an inspiring keynote presentation, followed by a full day of talks and tutorial sessions.

The day will close with one final keynote, and then finish with by the evening's social event. Breakfast and lunch will be served and refreshments will be available throughout the day.

Tickets

Applied AI is a full featured conference and ticket prices include all of the following:

  • Entrance to the keynotes, tutorials and conference sessions
  • Entrance to the pre and post-conference social events
  • Breakfast, lunch and 2 cake & coffee breaks
  • All day access to the exhibit hall and delegate lounge
  • WiFi, charging points and cloakroom

Attending

Applied AI is a conference aimed for developers, data scientists and analysts that are creating software, applications, tools, or platforms that are based on or incorporate artificial intelligence or machine learning technologies.

Anyone is welcome to attend the conference, whether you're a professional, hobbyist, manager, or just starting out, there's something for everyone.

Applied AI operates a Code of Conduct and we encourage all delegates to read it before attending the conference.
