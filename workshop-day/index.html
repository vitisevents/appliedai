Workshop Day

Thursday 25th October 2018

Applied AI 2018 is pleased to bring to you two options for a full day of training on Thursday 25th October 2018, a day before the conference.

Both workshop options are a full day (9am-5pm) of hands on immersive training with top professional trainers. Tickets include lunch and coffee breaks. Please bring your laptop; power and wifi will be available.

Our call for papers is currently open and exact workshop details will be announced as soon as they are available.
