By Developers, For Developers

Applied AI is a conference intended for developers, data scientists and analysts that are creating software, applications, tools, or platforms that are based on or incorporate artificial intelligence or machine learning technologies.

Our vision for Applied AI is to offer attendees a forum for learning and networking with the most up-to-date topics in artificial intelligence and machine learning, tailored specifically to the needs of developers.

We are pleased to bring the inaugural Applied AI conference to the London tech community - a 1 day conference packed with amazing talks, plentiful networking opportunities and great social events. We hope to see you there!
