Social events

Evening events before and after the conference
Meet fellow delegates

An important part of any conference is being able to network with other delegates and Applied AI is no exception. We will be arranging two social events - before and after the conference.

Thursday February 28th 2019 from 6pm - 9pm

Pre-Conference Social

Thursday night kicks off the conference with a night at the pub. Open to anyone with an interest in machine learning and artificial intelligence, we'll be meeting at Finch's Pub on Finsbury Square from 6pm for drinks, snacks. This is your chance to meet other delegates before the conference starts.

From 7.00pm our we'll have a speaker do a short talk about an AI-related subject. Drinks and chat will then continue after the presentation.

Finch's Pub

Friday March 1st 2019 from 5pm - 9pm

Post-Conference Social

Head to the Brewery's brand new event space The Grubstreet Author right after talks finish and join us for some snacks and drinks while networking with fellow attendees. There will be food served, including vegetarian and vegan options, and free drinks.

Please note: our Code of Conduct applies to all venues associated with the conference, including off-site social events.
