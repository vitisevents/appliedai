Socials

Evening events happening throughout the conference

An important part of any conference is being able to network with other delegates and Applied AI is no exception. We will be arranging two social events - before and during the conference.

Wednesday August 1st 2018 from 6pm

Wednesday night kicks off the conference with a night at the pub. We'll be meeting at Finch's Pub from 6pm til close for drinks, snacks and a chance to meet other delegates before the conference starts.

From 7pm our we'll have a speaker do a short talk about a Go-related subject. Drinks and chat will then continue after the presentation. The pre-conference social is open to anyone with an interest in Go.

Further details will be available closer to the date of the conference.

Thursday August 2nd 2018 from 6pm

Main Conference Social
Summer Party

Head outside to the Brewery courtyard right after talks finish and join us in the marquee for some dinner and drinks while networking with fellow gophers. There will be a buffet, including vegetarian and vegan options, and a few free drinks followed by a cash bar.

Please note: our Code of Conduct applies to all venues associated with the conference, including off-site social events.
