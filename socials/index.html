Social events

Evening events before and after the conference

An important part of any conference is being able to network with other delegates and Applied AI is no exception. We will be arranging two social events - before and after the conference.

Thursday October 25th 2018 from 6pm

Thursday night kicks off the conference with a night at the pub. We'll be meeting at pub close to the venue from 7pm til close for drinks, snacks and a chance to meet other delegates before the conference starts.

From 7.30pm our we'll have a speaker do a short talk about an AI-related subject. Drinks and chat will then continue after the presentation. The pre-conference social is open to anyone with an interest in machine learning and artificial intelligence.

Further details will be available closer to the date of the conference.

Friday October 26th 2018

Afterparty

Head to the main exhibition room right after talks finish and join us for some snacks and drinks while networking with fellow developers. There will be a hot and cold snacks, including vegetarian and vegan options, and a free drinks followed by a cash bar.

Please note: our Code of Conduct applies to all venues associated with the conference, including off-site social events.
